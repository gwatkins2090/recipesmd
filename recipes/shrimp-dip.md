# Shrimp Dip

## Ingredients

- 1 8 oz. carton sour cream
- 1 pkg. Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (dry)
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 can shrimp (drained & rinsed)

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients together and serve with crackers and/or chips.
