# Pecan Pie

## Ingredients

- 1 unbaked 9-inch pie shell
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup white Karo
- 4 Tbsps. Butter, melted
- 1 Tbsp Vanilla flavoring
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup pecans

## Instructions

1. Mix sugar, Karo, butter, vanilla and eggs. Pour over pecans in pie shell. Bake at 350Â° for 45 to 50 minutes.
