# Pecan Pie

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/3 cup butter, melted
- 1 cup dark corn syrup
- 1 cup pecan halves
- 1 unbaked 9" pie shell

## Instructions

1. Beat together with rotary beater: eggs, sugar, salt, melted butter, and dark corn syrup.

2. Mix in pecan halves.

3. Pour into pastry-lined 9" pan.

4. Bake at 375Â° for 40-50 minutes until set and pastry is nicely browned.

5. Cool before serving.
