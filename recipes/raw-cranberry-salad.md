# Raw Cranberry Salad

## Ingredients

- 2 Cups raw cranberries, ground
- 1 whole orange, ground
- 1 can crushed pineapple (8 oz)
- Â½ cup sugar
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 package raspberry gelatin
- 1 cup hot water

## Instructions

Dissolve gelatin in hot water. Mix all ingredients and pour into glass bowl (Congeal).
