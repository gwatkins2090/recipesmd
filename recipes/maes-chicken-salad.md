# Mae's Chicken Salad

## Ingredients

### Dressing
- ½ cup miracle whip
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 Tbl wor. Sauce

### Salad
- 2 cups chicken
- 1 cup celery
- 1 cup pecans
- 1 cup grapes
- ½ onion

## Instructions

Mix all together then add items below: 2 cups chicken, 1 cup celery, 1 cup pecans, 1 cup grapes, ½ onion.
