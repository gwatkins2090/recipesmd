## Recipe #106: Pecan Pie
*(Different version)*

#### Ingredients:
- Beat together with rotary beater
- 3 eggs
- ⅔ cup sugar
- ½ cup light syrup
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 1 cup dark corn syrup
- 1 cup pecan halves

#### Instructions:
Cream together - put in 9" pastry. Bake at 375° - nicely browned. Cool.

Temperature: 375°
Time: Bake 40-45'
*Note: Files very for flour*
