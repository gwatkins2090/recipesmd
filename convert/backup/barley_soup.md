## 18. Barley Soup

**Ingredients:**
- 1 table sp. peeled barley
- 1 stalk celery
- 3 green onions
- 3 table sp. oleo
- 1 tsp. sp. dried parsley flakes
- 4 cups water
- salt & pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
Wash barley and soak in water (covered) and slow heat for 2Â½ hours. Add other ingredients, except cabbage and milk and continue cooking over low heat for another hour. Add cabbage and cook another 30 min. Add milk and heat to boiling. Serve.
