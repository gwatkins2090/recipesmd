## Recipe #88: Breakfast Casserole

**Ingredients:**
- 6 slices white bread - crust removed
- 1/2 stick butter
- 1 1/2 cups grated cheese
- 6 eggs - well beaten
- 3 cups milk
- 1 pound sausage & ham
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp dry mustard
- Pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Cook sausage & ham, drain
2. Melt butter in 9 x 13 dish - tear bread into small pieces over butter. Sprinkle sausage over bread - sprinkle cheese over meat.
3. Beat eggs and other ingredients together. Pour over mixture until overnight. Bake at 350Â° for 40 or 50 minutes. Place foil over top to keep from browning too fast.
