## Recipe #114: Coconut Pie (Bayleys)

### Ingredients:
- 1½ c. sugar
- 3 eggs whipped
- 2 cups milk
- 2 tbs corn starch
- ½ stick butter
- ½ tsp vanilla
- 2 cups coconut

### Instructions:
Bring milk, butter & sugar to a boil. Add other ingredients - stir till cream. Add coconut.

Bake 15-20 - 425°
