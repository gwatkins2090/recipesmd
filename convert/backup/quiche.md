## 20. Quiche

**Ingredients:**
- 4 eggs
- 3 slices milk
- 1 cup biscuit mix
- ½ cup ham pieces
- ½ slice crumbled bacon (broken)
- 1 table spoon grated onion
- Salt & pepper

**Instructions:**
Beat eggs & milk & biscuit mix. Add other ingredients. Put in (?) pie shell and bake about 30 min at 375°.
