## 73. Salted Pecans (Express Printing)

**Ingredients:**
- 2 qt water
- 4 tabs. salt
- 6 cups pecans

**Instructions:**
Bring w t s to boil. Remove from heat add pecans and let set 10 min. Strain and spread on cookie sheet. Cook 2½ hr at 225°.
