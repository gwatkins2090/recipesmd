## 21. Broccoli & Rice Casserole

**Ingredients:**
- 1 package frozen chopped broccoli
- 1 can cream chicken soup
- 6 oz. Cheez Whiz
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 1 button garlic (small)
- 1 table sp. oleo

**Instructions:**
Cook rice - 10 min - Drain in colander - Mix with rice in mixing bowl. Saut√© garlic in oleo. Pour chilled & thawed soup & Cheez Whiz in oleo and mix well until Cheez is melted. Pour this over rice & broccoli and mix lightly - Put in casserole - Top with bread crumbles (with melted oleo over bread crumbles - Cook until bubbly and crumbs are light brown. (about 20-25 min.)
