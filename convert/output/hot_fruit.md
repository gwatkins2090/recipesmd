## Description

A warm, comforting fruit compote featuring peaches, pears, pineapple, and plums baked with brown sugar and butter. This versatile dish works as a brunch side, dessert, or holiday accompaniment to ham.

## Ingredients

- 1 large can sliced peaches
- 1 large can sliced pears
- 1 large can pineapple chunks
- 1 can purple plums
- Small jar red cherries (if desired)
- Drain all the above
- 1 sliced banana
- A few nuts
- 1 stick butter or oleo
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 small jar applesauce

## Instructions

1. Pour this over fruit and heat in 325 to 350 oven about 20 or 30 minutes.
